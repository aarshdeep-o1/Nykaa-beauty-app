Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your Nykaa account and visiting the 'My Orders' section. You'll also receive tracking information via email and SMS.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened products. For opened products, returns are accepted within 15 days if you're not satisfied with the quality.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within India only. We're working on expanding our international shipping options and will update you soon!

How can I become a Nykaa partner brand?

We're always looking for innovative beauty brands to partner with. Please reach out to us at partnerships@nykaa.com with your brand information and product details.

Are your products authentic?

Yes! We guarantee 100% authentic products. All our products are sourced directly from brands or their authorized distributors.

Do you have physical stores?

Yes, we have Nykaa stores in major cities across India. Visit our store locator on the website to find the nearest store to you.
